January 03, 2024

New Warewulf Slack workspace

The Warewulf project is moving its development and community chat to a dedicated Warewulf Slack workspace at warewulf.slack.com. If you're not already a member, you can join here or find an invite link at warewulf.org/help.

The new Slack workspace has channels for development, organization, help, and general discussion. There's even a warewulf3 channel for people who haven't moved to Warewulf v4 yet. And don't forget to stop by #introductions to tell us all about yourself and how you're using Warewulf!

We hope to see you there soon!
